Approval App (Jeeves ERP)

App created on demand and deployed to 5 large manufacturing companies with potential of buy out from Jeeves.

The approval App aims to approve documents through the web application connected to the Jeeves ERP system, was created for NordERP, and works within the intranet network. Thanks to the connection of approval with orders, users can track the entire financial expenses and investments cycle from the request phase to their payment.

Main responsibilities

  • Created the whole app on demand
  • Gathered requirements from NordERP
  • Created architecture, design and layout
  • Implemented windows auth with AD
  • Deployment process
